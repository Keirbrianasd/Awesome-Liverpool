# Contributing to Awesome Liverpool

Contributions are welcome! Please take a moment to review these guidelines before submitting a pull request.

## Criteria for Inclusion

To keep this list high-quality, we look for resources that are:
- **Liverpool-centric:** The resource must be specifically for or about Liverpool, UK.
- **Active:** Websites must be live, and businesses/events must be currently active.
- **Relevant:** Resources should provide genuine value to locals, visitors, or businesses.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository.**
2. **Add your resource** to the appropriate category.
3. **Alphabetize your entry** within that category by its name.
4. **Use the standard format:** `[Name](URL) - Short, objective description.`
5. **Check your links** to ensure they work.
6. **Submit a Pull Request** with a clear title (e.g., "Add [Resource Name] to [Category]").

## Important Note

We aim to keep descriptions objective. Avoid "marketing-speak" or overly promotional language. 

Thank you for helping make Awesome Liverpool better!
